 OK Sauce 

Glyn Thomas (mrglynthomas.com) associates the name OK Sauce to the things he releases into the sonic ether. The original Ok Sauce company created an authentic individual taste experience and was the largest employer in Southfields, with workers that probably lived in the Southfields home where I now try and create authentic multimedia experiences from, hence the name. Ok Sauce is a new sonic venture planning a first release in 2018.

Glyn Thomas lives in Southfields SW18, London.
